Cedric Gervais Enlists Willy Monfret On Deep House Tune Make Me Feel

September 24, 2024
blog

French producers Cedric Gervais and Willy Monfret teamed up on a track this week titled “Make Me Feel.” The new release sees the two producers crafting a monster of a deep house tune that’s perfect for late nights at the club.

Recommended Videos

“Make Me Feel” opens with a standard house intro, dominated by a pounding kick drum and shuffling hi-hats. Following an ethereal breakdown, Gervais and Monfret introduce a bouncy deep house bass accompanied by soulful vocals repeating the phrase that serves as the tune’s namesake. As if the catchy bass stabs weren’t enough to get crowds moving, the two producers implement a funky saxophone sample throughout the song that takes the energy to new heights.

Cedric Gervais and Willy Monfret truly take it to the next level on their latest effort with uplifting grooves and superior production values that form an unforgettable experience for the listener.

“Makes Me Feel” is available now via Armada Records.
