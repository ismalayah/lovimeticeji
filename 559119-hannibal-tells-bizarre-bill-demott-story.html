For those unaware, Bill DeMott used to be the head trainer of the Deep South Wrestling promotion, which was used as a training grounds for WWE talent.

In the video below, independent wrestler Hannibal describes an incident known as the “naked jelly donut stinkface” story, and includes a picture from Bill’s days as head trainer for WWE’s Deep South Wrestling developmental territory. Hannibal was involved in a very strange situation where Luke Gallows (Drew Hackinson, Festus, Fake Kane) was totally naked in the ring for a long period of time giving alternate naked stinkfaces to both Zack Ryder and female wrestler Melissa Coates while Bill held jelly donuts over their faces. The talent had agreed to do this with the encouragement of some of the other talent to get out of regular training that day.
